BOMBAY BIRYANI MASALA
Bombay Biryani Masala is a flavorful and aromatic spice mix used to prepare the iconic Bombay(Mumbai) Biryani, a dish that combines long-grain rice with marinated meat (usually chicken, mutton, or beef)
TANDORI MASALA
Tandoori chicken is a popular Indian dish known for its vibrant red color, smoky flavor, and tender texture. The chicken is marinated in a mixture of yogurt, spices, and herbs such as garam masala, cumin, coriander, turmeric, chili powder, and often garlic and ginger.
NIHARI MASALA
Nihari Masala is a traditional spice blend used to prepare Nihari, a slow-cooked, hearty stew originating from the Indian subcontinent, particularly associated with Mughlai and Awadhi cuisine.
